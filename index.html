ABOUT US

Path to Humanity

With the mission to provide the best medical services on a non-profitable basis by the people for the people, This Foundation Trust was established in 2004. For over a decade, the trust has worked hard towards the betterment of people and society. Currently, the Trust has invested and supported in numerous activities such as feeding poor people, sponsoring school education, providing educational aids and uniforms, medical and eye camp, rural women empowerment, and awareness projects, helping to destitute and orphan children and building old age homes. We work closely with people hence we understand their need and suffering. It is our belief that by contributing and supporting the people in need, we can make the world a better place to live in.


Mission

To Provide Best, Medical Social Services On A Non-Profitable Basis By The People For The People.


Vision

To Equip People With High Quality Resources Across The Nation.
